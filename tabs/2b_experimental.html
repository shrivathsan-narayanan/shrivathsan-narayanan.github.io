Fan-array Wind Generator and Rotor Test Rig

A compact, open-channel, fan array wind generator (FAWG) facility has been developed to produce controlled gust flow conditions for aerodynamic testing in laboratory environments. A rotor test rig has also been designed and fabricated, to measure the aerodynamic loads and moments of isolated rotor systems subjected to diverse gust fields.
